An integration platform as a service (iPaaS) is designed to solve that problem. It provides a cloud-based environment where businesses can build, deploy, and manage integrations between applications, systems, and data sources, without maintaining complex infrastructure. In simple terms, iPaaS acts as a central hub that enables different software applications to communicate with each other seamlessly. Understanding the Basics At its core, an integration platform as a service is a cloud-hosted suite of integration tools.
