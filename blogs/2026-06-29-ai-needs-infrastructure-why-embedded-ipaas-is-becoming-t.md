The post argues that embedded iPaaS is becoming critical infrastructure for AI-powered SaaS, since even the most capable AI system is only as useful as the data it can access and act on. It highlights that while AI dominates headlines, the underlying integration technology enabling data access and action execution is increasingly essential for SaaS vendors embedding AI into their products.
